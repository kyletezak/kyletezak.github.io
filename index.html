My name is Kyle Tezak. I come from a long line of morticians. My family has been in the funeral business for over one hundred years. I'm the first graphic designer, to my knowledge. They try not to hold it against me.

I practice design and illustration in Chicago, Illinois. My specialties include interaction design, information architecture and UX, product design, branding, icon design, editorial illustration, creative direction, and consulting. I've even been getting my hands dirty in front-end development, for instance this site.

When it comes to design I like to keep things simple. And most of the time keeping things simple requires a lot of work. Design is as much about organizing information and crafting a message as it is about creating things to look at. A good designer is equal parts stategist, storyteller, artist, and nerd.
